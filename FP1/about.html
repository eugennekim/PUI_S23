Meet the Members!

NewJeans (뉴진스) is a 5-member girl group under ADOR and HYBE Labels.
The members consist of Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.

Hyein (해린) is a South Korean singer under ADOR. She is a member of the girl group NewJeans. ADOR: Jan 1, 2022-present. Aug 1, 2022.
